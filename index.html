Sasquatch Challenge

October 9th, 2021

La Grande, Oregon

Details

What?

The Sasquatch Challenge is a bit of friendly competition showcasing some of La Grande's best local trails.

$20 entry fee donated entirely to the Blue Mountain Singletrack Trails Club to support their work building and maintaining trails in MERA.


The Challenge:

Ride as many laps as you can, as fast as you can in 6 hours on the Sasquatch / Ricochet loop. If you hammer out one lap, that's awesome. If you go for 6 hours straight, excellent. Ride as much as you want.

Where?

The Owlsey Canyon trailhead at MERA (Mt. Emily Recreation Area)

When?

October 9th, 2021 10:00AM

  • 09:00AM: Registration opens
  • 10:00AM: Ride starts
  • 03:00PM: Cutoff for final lap

Why?

Because bikes are fun and the trails should be good again.
