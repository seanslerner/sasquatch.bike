Sasquatch Challenge

October 11, 2020

Details

What?

The Sasquatch Challenge is a bit of friendly competition showcasing some of La Grande's best local trails.


The Challenge:

Ride as many laps as you can - up to 6 - as fast as you can. If you can only hammer out one lap, that's awesome. We'll have rankings for fastest single lap up to 6 laps with solo and two-person team categories.

Where?

The Owlsey Canyon trailhead at MERA (Mt. Emily Recreation Area)

When?

October 11, 2020

  • 09:00AM: Registration opens
  • 10:00AM: Ride starts
  • 04:30PM: Cutoff for final lap
  • 05:00PM: After party at Side A

Why?

Because bikes are fun and the trails should be good again.
